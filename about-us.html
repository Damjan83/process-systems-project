About Us

Who are we and what can we do for you?

Process Systems has been at the forefront of the process control industry within Australia and the Pacific region since it was founded in Australia in 1989. Since then it has continued with one goal in mind; to give the best service possible to our customer base. We manufacture and distribute high quality process valves in the Australia and Pacific regions to a wide range of industries including mining, irrigation, water treatment, power, plumbing, government and other industries. Our range includes high quality solenoid valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, knife gate valves, check valves, strainers, pneumatics and many more ancillary products. With an office full of qualified engineers and many years of experience in the process control industry and an ISO 9001 approval you can be assured you are receiving the correct product at the right price.

We look forward to helping you find the right solution.
